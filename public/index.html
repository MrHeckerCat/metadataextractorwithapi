Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use this tool?
Enter the URL of an image in the input field and click "Check metadata" to extract the metadata.
What type of metadata can I find?
You can find information such as image size, creation date, camera settings, and more, depending on the image.
Is it possible to extract metadata from any image?
Most digital images contain some form of metadata, but the amount and type of information can vary depending on the image source and format.
